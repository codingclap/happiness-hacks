Mantra

Today, I release stress so I can be at peace.

Stress is harmful not only to your mental health but your physical health too. Think sleep loss, difficulty relaxing, and low self-esteem. Between work, relationships, money, and health concerns, there are no off-limit life areas that can trigger stress. Not sweating the small stuff sounds cute and all, but sometimes it’s easier said than done. The good news is that you can kick this beastly emotion to the curb and find peace with the right mindset.

5 Tips for Easing Stress

  • Deep yoga breathing.
  • Reset and unwind with a guided meditation before bed.
  • Talk it through with a trusted confidant or a Psychic.
  • Exercise to release feel-good endorphins.
  • If you can, take a day off for your mental health.
